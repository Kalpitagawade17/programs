FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about 'Tale By Tale'. If you have more, feel free to drop us a mail or directly contact us.



What's Tale By Tale?

Tale By Tale is a place designed for book lovers with your own personal webpage. Tale By Tale gathers book lovers, book bloggers, reviews, writers and readers from all over the world who can share their reviews and other writings, reading updates and find other interesting books, writers and readers


Is Tale By Tale independent?

Yes, it is 100% independent.


Is Tale By Tale free?

Yes, it is absoltely free.


What’s my personal address?

Your personal address includes your username and looks like this: yourusername.talebytale.com.


Can I login with my social media?

Yes, you can but first you need to connect your social media.


How to rate book?

To rate a book click on the cover, then select ratings stars.


Can I use half-stars rating?

Yes, you can.


I tweeted from my Kindle but nothing happened on my Tale By Tale account. What could go wrong?

Check if you've connected your Twitter account and switched ON Kindle sync.


How can I find my friends on Tale By Tale?

You can find your Facebook and Twitter friends if you connected your Facebook and/or Twitter account.


How can I see reviews of other Tale By Tale members?

You can find reviews wrote by other members by using Tag Search (Enter book title, select Tags and hit enter).


I tried to connect my BookLikes profile with my Facebook Page but nothing happened.

You can connect only your Facebook profile.


Can I auto-post my reviews to my social media?

Yes, you can. Once you connect your social media.


How can I post my review to Facebook or/and Twitter?

You can post your reviews to Tale By Tale, Facebook and Twitter at one go. Connect your social media acconts.


What if the review is just wrong?

Everyone does make mistakes. If the review contains large, factual errors, then please contact us.


Will I be guaranteed a good review?

Our reviewers are give honest, impartial evaluations of the books they receive. The resulting reviews can be positive, negative or anywhere in between. What we do guarantee is that you’ll receive a fair and unbiased review .
